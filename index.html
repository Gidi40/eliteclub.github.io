About Elite Club of Nigeria(Ngwa land)

Elite Club of Ngwa land is a Social/Cultural association based in Lagos. Membership is open to Ngwa origin and any other person that might have the interest of Ngwa land at heart.

Since inception, the club have gone through several dynamic changes which have made it one of the best non-political forum in Ngwaland. Though a non political group, Elite Club of Ngwa land have continued to play a key role in the politics of Abia state and Nigeria as a whole.

Why Join Us

The Club was formed with the following
aims and objectives

1.

To know and develop members, foster unity and understanding among each other.

2.

To cater for the interest of members and their family both far and near

3.

To help and direct people at home on social and economic issues as at when necessary.

4.

To donate to worthy causes and projects instituted by communities at home when necessary there by encouraging the development of the communities.

5.

To create an association for the enhancement of good relationship between members and non members.
